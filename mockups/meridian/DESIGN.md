# Meridian

## Concept

Editorial, refined, and light-first. Inspired by high-end publication design and academic typography. Serif headings paired with clean sans-serif body text. Generous whitespace signals confidence and clarity. The vibe is "thoughtful scientist who values craft."

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | `#FAFAF8` | Warm white primary background |
| Text Primary | `#1a1a1a` | Headings, names |
| Text Secondary | `#555555` | Body text, descriptions |
| Text Muted | `#999999` | Dates, category labels, metadata |
| Accent | `#C2452D` | Terracotta — links, underlines, markers |
| Border | `#E8E4DF` | Section dividers, subtle lines |
| Border Light | `#F0ECE8` | Sub-dividers within sections |

## Typography

- **Headings**: Serif stack (`Georgia`, `Times New Roman`, `serif`) — evokes academic publishing and editorial gravitas
- **Body/UI**: Sans-serif stack (`Helvetica Neue`, `Helvetica`, `Arial`, `sans-serif`) — clean and readable
- **Category labels**: Sans-serif, small caps with letter-spacing — quiet structural hierarchy
- **Tech stacks**: Sans-serif italic — distinguishes metadata from content
- **Dates**: Sans-serif, muted color, smaller size

## Layout

- Single column, max-width ~900px, centered
- **Whitespace is the primary design element** — 80-100px between sections, 40-60px between entries
- Full-width thin horizontal rules between sections
- Shorter partial rules between sub-entries (e.g., between job positions)
- Two-column layout only for skills section
- Strong left-alignment for all content
- No cards or boxes — content breathes on the open background

## Key Design Elements

- Serif headings create an editorial, authoritative feel
- Terracotta accent used sparingly — only on links, section underlines, and company names
- Short colored underline below each section title (50-80px wide)
- Social links displayed as text with `·` dot separators (not icons)
- Skills listed as plain text separated by `·` dots (not pills or tags)
- Projects separated by thin partial-width rules (not enclosed in cards)
- Tech stacks in italic to distinguish from descriptions
- Hero splits the name across two lines for dramatic scale
- Centered contact section as a clear visual conclusion

## Navigation

- Full name "Justin Bricker" on the left in serif (doubles as logo)
- Section links on the right in small caps sans-serif with letter-spacing
- Thin bottom border separating nav from content
- No background — transparent over the warm white
- Active link indicated by accent color, not background change

## Dark / Light Mode

- **Light-first design** — the warm white palette is the defining aesthetic
- Dark mode: background shifts to `#1a1a1a`, text to `#E8E4DF`, accent stays terracotta
- The editorial feel carries through both modes
- Dark mode gains a subtle warmth from the terracotta accent against dark gray

## Sections

- **Hero**: Very large two-line serif name, thin subtitle, text social links with dot separators, short accent-colored rule, brief intro paragraph
- **About**: Serif section heading with accent underline, generous spacing around two body paragraphs
- **Experience**: Entries separated by partial-width rules, serif role titles, accent-colored company names, dates in muted sans-serif
- **Skills**: Two-column layout, uppercase letter-spaced category labels, skills as dot-separated plain text
- **Projects**: Vertical list (not cards), serif titles, description lines, italic tech stacks, separated by light rules
- **Education**: Similar to experience — clean, minimal entries
- **Contact**: Centered, serif heading, accent-colored email link, text social links

## Mood / Inspiration

Communicates intellectual rigor, taste, and restraint. The serif typography and editorial layout say "I read papers and think deeply about problems." The warm color palette and generous whitespace feel approachable rather than cold. This design appeals to someone who values substance and craftsmanship — the kind of person who'd rather write a well-structured essay than a flashy slide deck.
